Greenshelf

Project Abstract

Greenshelf is an innovative cross-platform ecosystem designed to tackle the critical issue of household food waste, which accounts for 10 million tons of lost food annually in France. The project focuses on empowering users to manage their food inventory effectively through a user-centric mobile application and a supporting web infrastructure.

Technical Solution & Features

The application features a smart stock management system allowing for manual input or barcode scanning to log products and their expiration dates. To reduce cognitive load for the persona (Julie, a busy 40-year-old professional), the system integrates automated push notifications and a recipe suggestion algorithm based on real-time inventory.

Design & UX Strategy

The UI/UX design follows a "benevolent and non-judgmental" philosophy, utilizing an organic color palette (Leaf Green, Earth Brown) and clean typography (Lato, Open Sans) to ensure high accessibility and a soothing user experience.

Technical Stack: HTML/CSS landing page, WordPress showcase site for SEO, and an MVP currently in deployment on Bubble.

Economic & Environmental Impact

With a strategic €100,000 marketing budget focused on social media engagement (Instagram), the project aims for 50,000 downloads within three months.

Financial Saving Up to €240 per year saved per family by optimizing consumption.
Ecological Footprint Avoiding 150kg of CO2 emissions per household annually.
